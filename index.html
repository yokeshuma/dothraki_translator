Dothraki Translator

A Little About Dothraki

The Dothraki language is a constructed fictional language in George R. R. Martin's fantasy novel series A Song of Ice and Fire and its television adaptation Game of Thrones.

About The App

  • It's a English to Dothraki Translator.
  • Uses API from funtranslations.com & API rate limit is 60 calls per day with distribution of 5 calls per hour
